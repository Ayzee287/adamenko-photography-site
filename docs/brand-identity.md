# Brand Identity System — Adamenko Photography

**Date:** 2026-06-24
**Status:** v1 — derived from the approved homepage benchmark + brand-foundation. This is the **identity layer** (marks, logo, favicon, watermark, avatar, OG, type, colour, spacing, image rules). It does **not** redesign the site; it formalises the system the site already expresses and gives the owner usable assets.

> Companion docs: `docs/brand-foundation.md` (mission/positioning/voice) · `docs/image-guidelines.md` (export specs) · vault `04_Design/design-language.md` (tokens). Assets live in `public/brand/` + `app/icon.svg` + `app/apple-icon.svg`.

---

## 1. Brand in one line

**Adamenko Photography** — warm, documentary family · couple · maternity · wedding photography in Lyon, available across Europe. Premium *without* being aloof. The photographs carry the colour and emotion; the identity stays quiet and gets out of the way.

---

## 2. Logo & wordmark

The brand has **three lockups** plus a **monogram**. The website header deliberately renders the brand as **live text** (`site.brand` in Fraunces) for crisp responsive scaling and future localisation — so these SVGs are for **off-site** use (email signature, prints, photo watermark, partner/vendor listings, social headers).

| Asset | File | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Primary wordmark (light bg) | `public/brand/wordmark.svg` | Default — on paper / light surfaces |
| Wordmark (dark bg) | `public/brand/wordmark-on-dark.svg` | Over photographs / the dark contact band |
| Monogram "A" | `public/brand/monogram.svg` | Social avatar, stamp, tight spaces |
| Favicon | `app/icon.svg` (32px) | Browser tab — generated by Next |
| Apple touch icon | `app/apple-icon.svg` (180px) | iOS home-screen |
| Watermark | `public/brand/watermark.svg` | Discreet overlay on delivered/preview photos |

**Wordmark construction:** `ADAMENKO` in Fraunces 600, letter-spaced ~6, flanked by two short **clay rules**, with `PHOTOGRAPHY` beneath in Inter, letter-spaced ~11, muted. The clay rules are the one accent — they echo the site's "drawing underline" motif.

**Monogram / favicon:** an ink rounded-square, a paper serif **A**, and a small **clay underline** under the A. The underline is what makes it *this* brand and not a generic "A" — it ties the mark to the clay accent and the link-underline language. Reads cleanly at 16 px.

**Clear space & min size:** keep clear space ≥ the cap-height of `ADAMENKO` on all sides. Min wordmark width ~140 px on screen / 32 mm in print; below that, use the monogram.

**Don'ts:** don't recolour the mark outside the palette · don't add shadows/bevels/gradients · don't stretch · don't place the light wordmark on a busy photo without a scrim · don't substitute another serif.

## 3. Typography

| Role | Face | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Display / headings | **Fraunces** (400/500/600) | Warm humanist serif — soft, friendly, *not* a cold fashion didone. `--font-serif`. |
| Body / UI | **Inter** | Clean humanist sans. `--font-sans`. |
| Eyebrows | Inter, `text-xs uppercase tracking-[0.22em] text-muted` | The one canonical eyebrow rule. |

- **Display scale** lives in the homepage benchmark (hero → section H2 → page H1 via `PageHeader`). Headings use `text-balance`; body uses `text-pretty` with a constrained measure (`max-w-2xl`).
- **Fallbacks** (for the SVG assets, where the web font isn't loaded): `'Fraunces','Playfair Display',Georgia,serif`. Playfair is the closest free match to keep the off-site marks consistent.
- **Pairing rule:** never two serifs; never a competing display face. Fraunces + Inter only.

## 4. Colour

The disciplined warm palette (vault `design-language`, D006/D026). The chrome is near-neutral; the **photographs supply all saturation**.

| Token | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| `--color-ink` | `#2a2420` | Text, dark surfaces, the mark's ground. Never pure black. |
| `--color-paper` | `#faf6f0` | The signature surface. Never pure white. |
| `--color-muted` | `#6f655c` | Secondary text (WCAG-AA on paper + sand). |
| `--color-line` | `#e7ddd0` | Hairlines. |
| `--color-clay` | `#b07159` | The single accent — links, underlines, marks. **Never** buttons or gradients. |
| `--color-sand` | `#f3ece1` | Quiet warm band tint (newly tokenised — was hardcoded). |

**Accent discipline:** clay is a teaspoon, not a coat. It appears on link underlines, the eyebrow marks, the wordmark rules, the favicon underline — and nowhere as a fill. This restraint is what reads as *premium*.

## 5. Spacing & layout

One vertical rhythm, five tokens (base 0.25rem), no arbitrary values (`globals.css`):

```
XS 24px → 6   ·   S 40px → 10   ·   M 64px → 16   ·   L 96px → 24   ·   XL 128px → 32
FLOW  py-10 sm:py-16   (every standard section + inner page)
CLOSE py-16 sm:py-24   (the dark contact band only)
```

- **Container:** one `max-w-6xl` wrapper. Headings→content: `mt-10 sm:mt-16`. Inter-block: `space-y-16 sm:space-y-24`.
- **Inner pages now share FLOW** with the homepage (D030) — no page is "heavier" than the home sections.
- **De-boxed (D021):** structure comes from hairlines + whitespace + tone, **not** cards/borders. Imagery is the object.

## 6. Imagery principles

- **Image-first, UI-silent.** Borderless photographs (`ImageFigure`), no rings/cards. The interface frames the work, then disappears.
- **Documentary + natural light.** Real interactions over staged perfection; minimal posing; colour *and* black-and-white sequenced with intent.
- **Placeholder-first integrity.** Every reserved frame reads as a directed art-board (orientation·ratio + an art-direction note), never a "coming soon" gap; a real photo drops in with **zero layout shift** (CLS ≈ 0).
- **Ratios:** mixed, human edits (4:5 / 2:3 / 3:2 / 1:1 / 16:9). Hero 16:9, About portrait 4:5. Full spec: `docs/image-guidelines.md`.
- **Privacy:** no client names in filenames; consent before publishing; strip EXIF/GPS.

## 7. Social avatar & OpenGraph

- **Social avatar:** `public/brand/monogram.svg` (export to 1000×1000 PNG for platforms that need raster). Consistent across Instagram, Facebook, Google Business.
- **OG / share card (current):** an on-brand typographic card is generated automatically (`lib/og.tsx` → `app/opengraph-image` + `twitter-image`), 1200×630, dependency-free. No action needed for launch.
- **OG upgrade (post-launch):** swap to a **photo-backed** card once a signature frame is chosen + consent-cleared (`docs/image-guidelines.md` §8). Keep the subject centre-safe; add a scrim if overlaying the wordmark (use `wordmark-on-dark.svg`).

---

## 8. Voice (pointer)

Warm, plain, first person ("je" → "vous"). Short sentences, concrete nouns, few adjectives. **Say:** real moments · natural light · the way you are together. **Never say:** capturing memories · timeless · freezing moments · magical · stunning. Full spec: `docs/brand-foundation.md` §4.

| Element | Lives in |

| Favicon / app icon | `app/icon.svg` · `app/apple-icon.svg` |

Change the brand by changing tokens + content — never by adding chrome to components.
