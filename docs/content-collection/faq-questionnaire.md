# Content Collection — FAQs

**Goal:** capture the questions clients actually ask **before booking**, so the FAQ removes friction honestly. Goes into `content/faq.ts` (categories: `reservation`, `seance`, `livraison`, `deplacement`, `approche`).

> Draft answers already exist in `content/faq.ts`. Use this to (a) confirm those answers sound like you, and (b) add anything missing.

---

## How to use this

1. Skim the prompts below.
2. For each that's relevant, **write the answer in your own words** (2–4 sentences).
3. Add any **real question clients ask you repeatedly** that isn't listed.
4. Flag any draft answer you want changed.

---

## Reservation & booking
- How do clients book? What's the first step?
- How far ahead should they reserve (séance vs mariage)?
- Is a deposit required? Cancellation/reschedule policy? *(only if you have one)*
- Do you hold dates?

## The session
- Where do sessions happen (home / outdoors / studio)?
- How long does a typical session last?
- What should clients wear / bring / prepare?
- What if there are young children or a reluctant partner?
- What if the weather is bad (outdoor)?

## Your approach
- How would you describe your style? *(documentary, natural light, minimal posing)*
- Do you do posed photos at all?
- Colour or black-and-white — how do you decide?
- What if we feel awkward in front of the camera?

## Delivery
- When do clients receive the photos (preview + full gallery)?
- How are they delivered (private online gallery, resolution)?
- How many photos roughly?
- Are prints / albums available?
- Are RAW/unedited files available? *(usually no — state your policy)*
- What usage rights do clients get?

## Travel & coverage
- Where do you travel? (Lyon / France / Europe / international)
- How are travel costs handled?
- Do you work abroad / for destination weddings?

## Pricing (optional)
- Do you want any pricing info public, or "sur demande"?
- What's included in a typical session/package?

| Field | Example |
