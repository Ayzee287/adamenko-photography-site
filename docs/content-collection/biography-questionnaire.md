**Goal:** gather what's needed to write an authentic, documentary-voiced bio (no clichés). Answer in your own words — bullet points are fine. The developer turns your answers into `content/photographer.ts` (`biography`), confirmed with you.

> Voice reminder: warm, plain, first person. We avoid "capturing memories / freezing moments / timeless / passionate about". Specific and honest beats grand.
